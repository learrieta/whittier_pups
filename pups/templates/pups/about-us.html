About Us

Whittier Pups is a family run business by Jason and Jennifer Whittier and our 6 children located in Western MA (Hampden County). Jason and Jennifer grew up with dogs and we knew when we had kids that we wanted our kids to experience the special bond between a child and a dog. We fell in love with the Labrador breed after researching what would be the best fit for our family. Labradors are gentle and friendly with children and adults, eager to please, and overall a terrific addition to any family dynamic.

WHAT WE DO WHY WE DO IT HOW WE DO IT
At Whittier Pups, we breed Labradors with care and special attention given to healthy and socialized puppies perfect for any individual or family. We love our three Labradors! They have been terrific additions to our family and we are excited to share that joy with others. We have one male and two female Labradors that are healthy and well taken care of. We breed our dogs in a healthy and safe environment.

Our Dogs

Our labs are our own personal pets that we have had the honor of raising. We feel so blessed to have this wonderful Labrador breed in our life. Our labs have brought us so much joy, love and happiness. They are more than just another dog, our Labradors and their offspring are part of our family. In addition, we hope they will become be part of yours too!

AKC Profile

We are a small (despite having 6 kids and number 7 on the way) family run breeder and we want to keep it that way. Our goal is to provide a special companion to each one of our forever homes. Jason is a full time middle school ESL teacher and Jennifer is a stay at home mom. Our children range in age from 15 to 2 years old. Your new family member will have lots of hands on experience with kids and will come well socialized! We also have 2 cats that our puppies are always curious about.
